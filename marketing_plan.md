## 1. Pricing Strategy — The Coffee Strategy ☕

**Standard Price: $5.99/month per PC ($71.88/year)**

### Simple "All-Access" Model
- No tiers, no "pro" versions, no confusion.
- One price unlocks **every single tool** (Rebar automation, Roof framing, Visibility tools, etc.).

### Why $5.99/month is a Killer Price Point

| Advantage | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| ☕ **"Less than a coffee"** | Instantly relatable, removes all price objection |
| 🧠 **Low decision threshold** | Managers approve $5.99/month without a procurement process |
| 📈 **Volume play** | At $5.99/month you'll attract 10× more users than at $25/month |
| 🔒 **Habit forming** | Once teams rely on 100+ tools daily, $5.99/month is never cancelled |
| 💰 **Premium over $5** | The extra $0.99 earns ~20% more revenue than flat $5 |

### Trial Period

- **14-day full access free trial** — Autodesk App Store supports trial periods natively.
- Let users experience the full power of all 100+ tools before committing.

## 5. Differentiation Messaging

When marketing, hammer these messages everywhere:

1. **"$5.99/month. Less than a coffee. More than 100 tools."** — This is your headline. Use it in every ad, every social post, every email. The contrast between a trivial price and massive value is irresistible.
2. **"Your coffee is gone in 5 minutes. antiGGGravity saves you 5 hours a week."** — Emotional, relatable, and makes the ROI immediately obvious.
3. **"Built by structural engineers, for structural engineers"** — Most Revit toolkits are generic. antiGGGravity's rebar engine is the primary feature, not an afterthought.
4. **"Skip one coffee this month. Get 100+ professional tools forever."** — "Forever" (as long as subscribed) makes $5.99 feel like a steal.
5. **"100+ commands in a single add-in"** — Users save money compared to buying 5 separate niche plugins.
6. **"True Multi-Level Automation"** — Detailing columns and walls across 40 storeys in one operation. antiGGGravity manages the stack splicing and starter bars automatically.
7. **"Revit 2022 through 2027 — one license, all versions"** — Firms running mixed Revit environments get full coverage.
8. **"One-click installer, zero configuration"** — Ready to use in under 60 seconds. No support paths, no manual file copying.
