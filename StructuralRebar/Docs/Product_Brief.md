# antiGGGravity — Product Vision Brief

## The Product
A full-suite Revit add-in for structural engineers — built by a structural engineer, not a software company.

**Modules planned:**
- ✅ Structural Rebar (columns, walls, beams, foundations) — *advanced state*
- 🔲 Roof framing automation
- 🔲 Bracing tools
- 🔲 Drafting team productivity boosters
